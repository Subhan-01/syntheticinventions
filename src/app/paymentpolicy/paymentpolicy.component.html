Our Payment Policies

Payment Policy

We offer flexible, interest-free installment plans for payments. Clients can choose to pay weekly or monthly. For graphic design projects, 20% upfront payment is required. Web development projects require a 30-35% upfront payment.

Refund Policy

Refunds are considered on a case-by-case basis, depending on the project stage and scope.

Portfolio Policy

Some portfolio items displayed on our site are used as examples and will be updated with completed work as new projects are finalized.
